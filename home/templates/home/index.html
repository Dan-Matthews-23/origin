Welcome to Origins

In an alternate Earth, four powerful factions struggle for survival. The result: a cold war unlike anything in history. One small spark could ignite the fuse. You are the deciding factor. But who will you choose?


Will it be the Spartans, with their powerful militaries and ruthless rule?


Will it be the Amazons, experts in espionage and secrecy?


Will it be the Atlantians, with unrivaled defense?


Will it be the Witches, with endless economic capabilities?


Origins is a text-based Massive Multiplayer Online Role Playing Game, where you assume control of one of four factions amongst a world of nations. Each faction has a unique advantage.

Spartans

  • The Spartans rule by force. They are conquerors, unmatched in their attacking power. Under the King Gunter of Sparta, you will rule your faction with one goal: the complete destruction of anyone that oposes you.

  • Bonus: +25% attack power

Amazons

  • The Amazons gain strengh in secrecy and intelligence, and are natural-born enemies of the Spartans. Ruling from their secret city Ephesus, the Amazons use their intelligence network to watch from the shadows, waiting for the right time to strike. The Primary needs you now. Tread carefully.

  • Bonus: +25% intelligence power

Atlantians

  • Once defeated by a combined army of Amazons and Spartans, the Atlantians sunk their city to bide their time and rebuilt their forces. Their defences are all but impenetrable.

  • Bonus: +25% defence power

Witches

  • Loathed by all other races, the witches use their knowledge of magic to conjure materials they need for the war effort. Under the Grand Supreme, they have flourished, and you have been given command of a faction to take the fight to the enemy. Take your rightful place amongst kings and queens.

  • Bonus: +25% income power
